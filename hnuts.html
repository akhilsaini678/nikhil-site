11.Hazel nuts



Hazelnuts are very nutritious . One ounce (28 grams) of hazelnuts contains roughly:


Calories: 176
Fat: 9 grams
Protein: 6 grams
Carbs: 6 grams
Fiber: 3.5 grams
Vitamin E: 37% of the RDI
Magnesium: 20% of the RDI


Like many other nuts, hazelnuts appear to have beneficial effects on heart disease
risk factors.


One study found that a hazelnut-rich diet reduced total cholesterol, “bad” LDL
cholesterol and triglycerides. It also lowered markers of inflammation and improved blood vessel function .


Other studies have shown that hazelnut diets can improve cholesterol levels and
increase the amount of vitamin E in the blood.
